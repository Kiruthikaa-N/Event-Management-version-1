Meet Lauren



Lauren, the founder and expert event planner, boasts a wealth of experience in the industry, having spent almost two decades perfecting her craft. Her exceptional skills have led her to establish Fox Events in 2013, where she manages a flourishing team and provides an unparalleled level of service to her clients.
